General Concept Vision Center Kenya offices

Vision Center Kenya offices constitues a total of 13 offices accross the country. Of these, nine are field offices with each reporting to either of 3 regional offices, i.e. Three field offices report to 1 regional office.

The 3 regional offices report to the head office in Nairobi.

    The report handed to the head office includes the following items:
  • The area of coverage of each field office
  • The number of staff within an office
  • Issues the office is addressing in the area

Basically, the regional office is made up of a collection of 3 field offices. the head office brings together the regional offices.
